eaTr was created because meal planning is a pain. From coming up with a list of things you want to eat for the week, to making sure you don't forget any ingredients on your shopping list.

eaTr is a recipe book, meal planning app and shopping list app rolled into one.

eaTr makes everything as simple as possible. Store your favorite recipes, then with a click you can add the ingredients to your shopping list, making meal planning a breeze!
